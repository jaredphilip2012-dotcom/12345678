Quorzyx.com Community Guidelines

1. Share Experience, Not Guarantees

Post what you personally did and what happened. Do not make promises like "guaranteed returns" or "risk-free profit."

2. Be Honest and Transparent

If you hold a position in an asset you mention, disclose that clearly. No fake performance claims.

3. No Financial Advice Solicitation

Do not offer paid advising, portfolio management, or regulated services through this platform.

4. No Market Manipulation or Illegal Content

No pump-and-dump coordination, insider trading discussion, threats, scams, or unlawful activity.

5. Respectful Conduct

No harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or doxxing. Critique ideas, not people.

6. Admin Rights

The site operator may remove content or restrict access for any guideline or legal violation.
